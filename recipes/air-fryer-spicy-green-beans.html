Air Fryer Spicy Green Beans

Home

Description

Spicy green bean dish prepared in the air fryer. Total cook time of about 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 12oz trimmed green beans
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 glove garlic minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 F
  2. Place green beans in a bowl. Whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, garlic, and red pepper flakes in a separate bowl and pour over green beans. Toss to coat and let marinate for 5 minutes.
  3. Place half the green beans in the air fryer basket. Cook 12 minutes, shaking basket halfway through cooking time. Repeat with remaining green beans.
